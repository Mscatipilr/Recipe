Step by Step Directions for Making Banana Nut Bread

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a medium bowl, mash bananas.
  3. Add sugar and mix well.
  4. Add 1/2 cup flour and eggs, mix well.
  5. Add remainder of flour and baking soda, mix well.
  6. Add butter and nuts, mix well.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until well done.
