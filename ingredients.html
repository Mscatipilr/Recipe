Ingredients List for Banana Nut Bread

Your favorite Sunday morning comfort food.

  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups flour (flour is used in 2 steps)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2cups nuts
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cube margarine

When you've gathered your ingredients, you're ready to head to directions and start cooking!
