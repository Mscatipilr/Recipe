Banana Nut Bread

This recipe is a family tradition. Not only does this make a delicious, sweet, and savory bread but it brings me and my family joyful memories.
Banana nut bread is so easy to make. It only takes a few ingredients. We haven't make this recipe when we have overripe bananas but no need to wait! It's worth going ahead and buying some bananas just to make this bread! So, here we go!
